# Teacher Instructions: Lesson Plan Preparation

## üìã Overview
The school has enabled **Bulk-Only Mode** for lesson plan preparation. This means you must prepare **all sessions of a chapter together** instead of preparing individual sessions.

---

## ‚úÖ How to Prepare Lesson Plans (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Navigate to Lesson Planning
1. Log in to the app
2. Click on **"Lesson Planning"** or **"Scheme & Lesson Planning"** from the menu

### Step 2: Select Your Approved Scheme
1. You'll see a list of your **approved schemes** (schemes that have been reviewed by the headmaster)
2. Click on the scheme you want to prepare lesson plans for
3. The scheme will expand to show all chapters

### Step 3: Prepare All Sessions Together
1. **Find the chapter** you want to prepare
2. You'll see a button: **"Prepare All Sessions Together"**
3. **Click this button** OR **click on any session number** - both will open the bulk preparation form
   - Note: Individual session preparation is now disabled
   - Clicking any session will automatically redirect you to prepare all sessions together

### Step 4: Fill in the Bulk Preparation Form
A form will appear with the following sections:

#### üìÖ Session Scheduling
For **each session** in the chapter, you'll need to select:
- **Date**: When will you teach this session?
- **Period**: Which period of the day?

#### üìö Chapter-Level Details (Fill once for all sessions)
- **Learning Objectives**: What should students learn by the end of this chapter?
- **Teaching Methods**: How will you teach this chapter? (e.g., Lecture, Discussion, Practical)
- **Learning Resources**: What materials will you use? (e.g., Textbook, Videos, Lab equipment)
- **Assessment Methods**: How will you assess student understanding? (e.g., Quiz, Assignment, Project)
- **Differentiation Strategies**: How will you support different learning levels?
- **ICT Integration**: What technology will you use? (optional)
- **Cross-curricular Links**: Connections to other subjects? (optional)

#### üìù Session-Specific Details
For **each session**, provide:
- **Session Name/Topic**: Brief title for the session
- **Activities**: What specific activities will happen in this session?
- **Homework**: Any homework for this session?
- **Notes**: Additional notes (optional)

### Step 5: Review and Submit
1. **Review all the information** carefully
2. All required fields must be filled
3. Click **"Submit All Sessions"**
4. You'll see a success message confirming submission

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Rules

### ‚ùå What You CANNOT Do:
- **Cannot prepare single sessions individually**
  - Clicking on a session number will automatically redirect you to prepare all sessions together
  - This ensures complete chapter planning
  
- **Cannot skip sessions**
  - You must prepare all sessions in the chapter together

### ‚úÖ What You CAN Do:
- Prepare all sessions of a chapter together
- Click either "Prepare All" button OR any session number (both work the same)
- Choose different dates and periods for each session
- Provide detailed pedagogical information
- Save time by filling chapter-level information once

## üí° Tips for Success

### 1. **Plan Ahead**
- Look at your timetable before selecting dates and periods
- Make sure you select realistic dates based on your schedule

### 2. **Be Thorough**
- All required fields must be filled for submission to work
- Provide detailed learning objectives and teaching methods
- Think about assessment strategies upfront

### 3. **Use the Calendar View**
- Select dates that align with your teaching schedule
- Avoid holidays and exam periods

### 4. **Check Your Schemes**
- Only **approved schemes** will appear in the list
- If you don't see a scheme, it hasn't been approved by the headmaster yet

### 5. **Session Sequencing**
- Sessions are taught in order (Session 1 ‚Üí Session 2 ‚Üí Session 3...)
- Make sure dates are in chronological order

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Clicking session number opens bulk form"
- **This is normal behavior**: Single session preparation is disabled, so clicking any session automatically opens the bulk preparation form for the entire chapter

### Problem: "Can view but not edit planned sessions"
- **This is normal behavior**: When clicking a session that already has a lesson plan, you'll see a view-only modal showing all the details. To edit, you'll need to prepare all sessions together again.

### Problem: "Scheme not found in list"
- **Solution**: Your scheme hasn't been approved yet. Contact the headmaster for approval.

### Problem: "Missing required fields"
- **Solution**: Scroll through the form and fill all fields marked with *

### Problem: Can't select a date or period
- **Solution**: 
  - Check if the date is a holiday
  - Check if you already have a lesson plan for that date/period
  - Try a different date

### Problem: Form doesn't submit
- **Solution**: 
  - Check console for errors (press F12)
  - Make sure all required fields are filled
  - Try refreshing the page and submitting again
