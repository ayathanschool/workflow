# Fund & Expense Management - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### For Teachers

#### Create a Fund Collection Request
1. Navigate to **Fund Collection** in the menu
2. Click **"Create Request"** tab
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Class**: e.g., "Class 5A"
   - **Purpose**: e.g., "Athletic Meet Registration"
   - **Amount per Student**: e.g., 150
   - **Start/End Date**: Collection period
4. Wait for students to load
5. Select students using checkboxes (or "Select All")
6. Review total amount
7. Click **"Create Request"**
8. Go to **"My Requests"** tab
9. Click **"Submit for Approval"**

✅ **Done!** Admin will be notified via email.

#### Submit an Expense Claim
1. Navigate to **Expense Management**
2. Click **"New Request"** tab
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Amount**: e.g., 500
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown
   - **Reason**: e.g., "Transportation for field trip"
   - **Expense Date**: When expense occurred
   - **Receipt URL** (optional): Link to Google Drive receipt
4. Click **"Create Request"**
5. Go to **"My Expenses"** tab
6. Click **"Submit"**

✅ **Done!** Admin will review and approve.

---

### For Admin/HM

#### Approve a Fund Collection Request
1. Navigate to **Fund Collection**
2. Click **"Approve Requests"** tab
3. Review pending requests
4. For each request, you can:
   - **✅ Approve**: Click green "Approve" button
   - **✏️ Request Revision**: Click orange "Request Revision" button, provide notes
   - **❌ Reject**: Click red "Reject" button, provide reason
5. Confirm your action

✅ **Done!** Teacher and Accounts will be notified.

#### Approve an Expense Request
1. Navigate to **Expense Management**
2. Click **"Approve"** tab
3. Review pending expenses
4. Click **"View Receipt"** if available
5. Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**

✅ **Done!** Accounts will process payment.

---

### For Accounts Department

#### Acknowledge Collected Funds
1. Navigate to **Fund Collection**
2. Click **"Accounts"** tab
3. View **"Pending Acknowledgments"** section
4. Review expected vs. collected amounts
5. Click **"Acknowledge"** button
6. Confirm acknowledgment

✅ **Done!** Teacher will be notified.

#### Disburse an Expense
1. Navigate to **Expense Management**
2. Click **"Disburse"** tab
3. View **"Pending Disbursements"** section
4. Click **"Disburse"** button
5. Fill in payment details:
   - **Payment Mode**: Cash/Cheque/UPI/Bank Transfer
   - **Reference**: Transaction ID or Cheque number
6. Click **"Confirm Disbursement"**

✅ **Done!** Teacher will be notified.

## 📋 Status Guide

### Fund Collection Status
| Status | Meaning | What's Next |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Not yet submitted | Teacher submits for approval |
| **Pending** | Awaiting admin review | Admin approves/rejects/revises |
| **Revised** | Needs teacher changes | Teacher modifies and resubmits |
| **Approved** | Ready to collect | Teacher starts collection |
| **In Progress** | Collection ongoing | Teacher completes collection |
| **Completed** | Funds collected | Accounts acknowledges |

### Expense Status
| Status | Meaning | What's Next |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Not yet submitted | Teacher submits for approval |
| **Pending** | Awaiting admin review | Admin approves/rejects |
| **Approved** | Ready for payment | Accounts disburses funds |
| **Disbursed** | Payment complete | Closed |
| **Rejected** | Not approved | Closed |

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I edit a request after submitting?**
A: No, only drafts can be edited. If changes are needed, admin can request revision.

**Q: What if I collected less than expected?**
A: Enter the actual collected amount. Accounts will see and acknowledge the difference.

**Q: How do I upload a receipt?**
A: Upload to Google Drive, get shareable link, paste in Receipt URL field.

**Q: Can I delete a request?**
A: Yes, but only drafts. Once submitted, requests cannot be deleted.

**Q: When will I receive payment?**
A: After admin approval, accounts will disburse within 1-2 business days.

**Q: What categories are available for expenses?**
A: Travel, Materials, Professional Development, Books, Events, Technology, Office Supplies, Student Activities, Other.
